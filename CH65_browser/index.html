CH65 combinatorial landscape

Combinatorial landscape for CH65 influenza bnAb. Work by Angela Phillips and Daniel Maurer. Browser created by Milo Johnson (milo.s.johnson.13 [at] gmail.com) and edited by Thomas Dupic (dupic.thomas [at] gmail.com).

Tips

  • Click on C next to mutations to color the landscape according to these mutations (up to three)
  • Click on Δ next to a mutation to color the landscape according to the effect of the mutation on this specific background (red/blue increased/decreased affinity respectively)
  • Click on a mutation to only show the variants that have it
  • Use left/right arrow to move between variants
